Who we are

We are a group of art enthusiasts that came together with the idea of the artican creations and we are hellbent on achieving our mission which is to develop an art and talent building organization that supports and helps artists explore their talents thus sustaining themselves.

Our Phylosophy

We believe in teamwork and being empowered as youths. We also believe in trust of each other and our sponsors which always pays off. We also believe in sharing our little with the community and everyone at large.

How we work

We each have a talent and we are grouped into different categories where aur talents fit and together we grow each other in the arts production stage and that leads to our greater output. we also engage in farming,(rabit and onion farming just to mention a few) which in turn provides for us as well as the community around us.
